"Customer agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Vendor from any and all claims arising from Customer's use of the Services, without limitation."
No mutual indemnity. Playbook requires reciprocal language and a cap at 12 months of fees. Common in early-stage DPAs but unusual at Series B.

"Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time without cause upon 7 days' written notice."
7-day termination is below the 30-day floor. May trigger renegotiation downstream.
